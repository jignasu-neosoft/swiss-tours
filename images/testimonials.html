“The most amazing part of the trip was the Swiss travel pass & Stanserhorn was the best”

We had a lovely trip to Switzerland. Thanks to your help!! The apartment in Lucern was very nice. It had all the basic amenities and was comfortable. The most amazing part of the trip was the Swiss travel pass. it really helped us get the most out of our vacation. We took innumerable trains and booat trips and did not have to think twice about going anywhere. In fact, at times, we would just get off from the train at a beautiful place and then take the boat back to Lucerne. Unlike what I had I heard, Swiss people were very friendly and we thoroughly enjoyed the trip. Of the trips you had arranged for us, Stanserhorn was the best. It was not crowded and the views from the top were breathtaking. Mount Pilatus was also really nice. We enjoyed the boat trip and the train ride to the top. The cable ride back was amazing. Mount Titlis was very crowded and though the views from the top very truly beautiful, the crowd at the top took away a bit of the magic. The Swiss miniature was cute, but since we had already seen the mini Europe in Brussels, we felt we could have avoided it. All in all, this trip was amazing and we'll definitely like to go back to Switzerland! Hope to get in touch with you again then. Thanks for everything
